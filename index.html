Overview

Welcome to the Philippine Nikkei Jin Kai International School (PNJKIS), where we strive to provide an exceptional educational experience that fosters intellectual growth, cultural appreciation, and global citizenship. Our school is committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

History

Founded in June 1992, PNJKIS has a rich history of excellence in education. Over the decades, we have remained dedicated to our mission of providing high-quality learning opportunities that empower students to reach their full potential. From our humble beginnings to our present-day achievements, our commitment to academic excellence and student success has remained unwavering.

Mission & Vision

Our mission at PNJKIS is to cultivate a community of lifelong learners who are intellectually curious, culturally aware, and socially responsible. We strive to provide a holistic education that emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration. Our vision is to inspire individuals who are empowered to make positive contributions to their communities and the world at large.
